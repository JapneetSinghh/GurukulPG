About Us

Welcome to Gurukul PG for boys, a clean and furnished Paying Guest with a focus on providing Safety, Affordability, and Comfort.

Students and professionals traveling to Delhi for education, training, or job can stay in the best accommodations, thanks to Gurukul PG for Boys. Former students have said that Gurukul PG is like another home away from home for us. In Uttam Nagar, Ramdutt Enclave, and Vikaspuri, we offer the best paying guest services for boys.


According to your budget, we provide single and double-sharing accommodations. Gurukul PG provides homely food with quality accommodation for boys at an affordable price. Having years of experience, we have been modernizing our services as per the requirements of guests to provide superior services. The hostel facility is mostly suitable for long-term stays, offering three-course proper meals ( breakfast, lunch, and dinner ) with revised menus every week

Gurukul PG is a reasonably priced option that offers all the facilities you need. A few of the benefits that are included in the total rent are air conditioning as per your preference, fully furnished large rooms (including a study table, chair, and closet), high-quality furniture, wholesome cuisine, unrestricted high-speed WiFi, RO, laundry, and flawless housekeeping. Aakash Institute, Physics Wallah, Naraina, Target, Ramjas College, Kirorimal College, and other DU colleges are also nearby in Janakpuri.
We plan to provide our residents with a balanced, healthy lifestyle! We have you covered when it comes to cleanliness, eating habits, lifestyle, and emergencies. To provide you peace of mind, we have our guards continually up. We have everything, from PG wardens to more advanced security measures including lock systems and CCTV cameras.
